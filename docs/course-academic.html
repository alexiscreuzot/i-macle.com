  Academic Courses

Do you want to speak fluently in a different language?

If so, you’ve come to the right place! We have four languages to choose from: English, French, Spanish and German. We offer academic courses for teenagers and adults from beginner to advanced levels (A1-C2).

  • Six to eight students in each class.
  • Lots of speaking practice during each class to improve your pronunciation, vocabulary and fluency.
  • Experience total immersion by speaking to your teacher and classmates, watching videos, listening to audios, and reading.
  • You will follow a study plan that will help you to improve and achieve a higher level.
  • You will have extra, specialized support to improve your grammar and writing skills.
  • Dynamic learning environment that will include playing games and doing virtual exercises.
  • You will receive a Macle Institute certificate of completion at the end of the course.
  • Excellent opportunity to achieve complete mastery of the language of your choice and speak like a native speaker.

If you do not know your current level of the language you want to learn, we will send you a free placement test.
