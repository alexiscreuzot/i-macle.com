  Courses for kids between the ages of six and twelve

Do you want your child to be multilingual and achieve more academic and professional success than other children?

Studies have shown that it’s usually easier for young children to learn a new language than it is for teenagers and adults. Children who start learning a new language at a young age are more likely to:

  • Achieve fluency and communicate effectively with people from all over the world.
  • Receive scholarships to attend the best universities in the world, participate in international exchange programs, and obtain grant money and fellowships.
  • Gain access to the international job market, which usually leads to a very high salary.
  • Start their own company and expand its operations around the world.

Your child will enjoy our classes much more than their classes at their elementary or middle school because our classes are much more dynamic and fun thanks to our unique teaching methods.

  • Six to eight students in each class.
  • A teacher will be present at all times.
  • Your child will improve their pronunciation and will develop exceptional speaking skills by using an interactive platform which features audio exercises, games and songs.
  • Your child will learn about geography, history,science, etc. – this will allow your child to learn the new language in an important and useful context and give them a tremendous advantage during their academic and professional careers.
  • Your child will receive a progress report at the end of each unit with their results, progress assessment and feedback.
  • Your child will receive a coursebook and workbook at no additional cost, and they will do virtual exercises using a digital platform during class.
  • Each student will receive a Macle Institute certificate of completion at the end of the course.
