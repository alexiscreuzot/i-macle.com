  Conversational Classes

You want to improve your conversational skills by talking to your teacher and students who have the same level as you?

If so, we have the perfect classes for you. Four languages to choose from: English, French, Spanish and German, with classes available for teenagers and adults at levels A2,B1, and B2.

  • Five to seven students in each class.
  • Opportunity to discuss interesting issues including current events, science, technology, history, art, business, education, entertainment, etc.
  • You will also have a chance to teach your classmates about your home country and culture if you’d like.
  • A teacher will attend each class to facilitate the conversations and answer questions.
  • In each class, you will analyze an article, video or podcast and answer some listening or reading comprehension questions, and afterwards, you will discuss your answers with your classmates.
  • You will be able to communicate in the language of your choice with confidence after having interesting and enjoyable conversations with your classmates.
  • You will take a test to evaluate your speaking skills.
  • You will be able to pass the speaking section of a certification exam more easily.
