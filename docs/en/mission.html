  Our mission

In our institution, each teacher has the same mission: carry out their classes under the action -oriented approach or action. The student must be able to perform an action.

The student has small tasks in class, missions and challenges about daily life situations. As it happens when you live abroad. In this way, the student gradually develops the ability to analyze, decipher and apply new knowledge in their social environment. On the other hand, we work with pedagogical sequences so that the student remembers in each class what the previous class saw. Each topic is related to the following: repetition is the basis for strengthening and systematizing the knowledge already acquired.

In addition, students improvise dialogues about their life, have conversations and act a social role (play play) with their classmates to function in real -life situations. This is essential since the student is a member of a society and must be able to communicate, act and interact with the other members of their environment. Our purpose is that you learn to speak, write, listen and read in the language you choose to work, travel, live abroad and/or for pleasure.

He speaks a foreign language to discover the world and understand it. Meet new people traveling or in your country. It works with international companies and is part of new real or virtual communities.


Carolina Ojeda

  Academic Director

  Master in Education and Training, Dalf, Pro Fle, TKT

Learning a language was the best decision of my life. I discovered French culture, I traveled throughout Europe, I tried unparalleled meals and I also received a salary in euros which allowed me to stay several years in France. I met new people From different cultures, even my husband. I adopted a new vision for my life: give the same opportunity to the students of this institute.

I started my career in 2013 by teaching French in public and private universities in Mexico. With my knowledge in French I decided to apply for a scholarship as a Spanish language assistant in France. I then pursued and obtain a Master's degree in language teaching methods.

This has allowed me to bring to Mexico the experience and knowledge of the Methodology of the Council of Europe for the teaching of foreign languages. Likewise, teaching all kinds of audiences has allowed me to improve my skills to direct the institute.
