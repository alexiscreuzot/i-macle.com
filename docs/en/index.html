  FAQ

If you have doubts, click them with our frequent questions


What is Macle?

+

Macle is a 100% online institute dedicated to language teaching (English, French, Spanish and German). We have an academic coordinator, direction and finance.
In our institute you will learn languages following recognized methods worldwide and you can prepare to obtain a certification.

Are teachers native?

+

We have native and non -native professors, all certified in the language they teach. All our teachers, both native and non -native go through a selection process with several filters. They also have higher studies and experience in language teaching.

Should I buy material?

+

No. We provide you with the material you need to learn: book, exercise and audios notebook to practice. This is at no cost.

How are classes?

+

In English, French, Spanish or German class we use documents such as videos, texts, conversations audios, interactive games, flashcards and special activities for continuous oral expression and interaction (conversation).
Teachers follow the book sequences to perform an evaluation. Each sequence (didactic unit) can last 4 to 6 classes, depending on the level and language.

What material do you use to teach?

+

In the English classes we have the Cambridge platform, which has given excellent results to millions of students around the world.

In French we follow the methods of Maison by Langues, a worldwide recognized editorial, which has won several awards for their teaching methods. In Spanish we follow the digital manuals of Campus Diffusion that give us access to the teaching of the Cervantes Institute, a public institution created in Spain in 1991 to universally promote the teaching, study and use of Spanish and contribute to the dissemination of Hispanic cultures in The exterior.
In German we follow the egg methods, which have been used for many years and have trained millions of students who have obtained very good results.

Do you deliver any constancy of study?

+

We give you a progress control with feedback in PDF format at the end of each unit and a diploma at the end of each level.

Can I obtain a certification at the end of a level?

+

We are not exam applicators to obtain certifications but we prepare you for free certifications.
In English we prepare you to obtain a Cambridge certification. These certifications do not expire that by obtaining one, you will get it for life.
In French we prepare you to obtain the DELF and Dalf that are the official diplomas that accredit your level of mastery of the French language, which are granted by France Éduction International by the French Ministry of National Education.

In Spanish we prepare you to obtain the DELE granted by the Cervantes Institute, by the Ministry of Education, Culture and Sports of Spain.
In German we prepare you to pass a Goethe-Institut exam, a cultural institution of the Federal Republic of Germany, dedicated to the dissemination of German language and culture in the world. It offers exams to prove your German level through German certificates (Goethe-Zertifikat) of all levels of the common European framework.
